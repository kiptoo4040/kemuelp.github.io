President Says

Equity leaders clubs are clubs found in all institutions of higher learning. They are made up of members who are alumnis of wings to fly programme or elp interns. Aim of this clubs is to bring together this young brains and be in a position to network, give but to the society and develop each other. This clubs get to work closely with the banks for the good of all members.

Being the president of this great chapter, i would say it was a great call to lead a bunch of intellects who are always there ready to help make a diffrence in the world.KeMU fraternity is more of a family to me that I have never taken for granted.

I would like to thank Equity Group Foundation for giving up this chance to make a diffrence in our selves as well as our society
